Our Story

Air Taxi Nepal was founded by Kushal Rayamajhi with one simple goal — to make domestic air travel affordable and accessible for every Nepali traveller. Finding the cheapest flights used to be time-consuming and confusing. We changed that by building a simple, fast, and reliable platform where you can book seats on Buddha Air, Yeti Airlines, and Shree Airlines in just a few clicks.

We serve customers from all across Nepal — whether you are travelling for work, family, or adventure. Air Taxi Nepal gets you there at the best price.

🛡️ Privacy Policy

Air Taxi Nepal Pvt Ltd · Kathmandu, Nepal · Last updated: June 7, 2026 · View full policy ↗

Air Taxi Nepal is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our services at www.airtaxinepal.org.

Information We Collect

When you use our service, we may collect:

  • Email address, first and last name, phone number
  • Usage data — IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent
  • Device identifiers and mobile operating system information

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain our flight booking service
  • Manage your account and process bookings
  • Contact you about updates, offers, and support
  • Improve our platform through analytics and usage trends

Cookies

We use session and persistent cookies to authenticate users, remember your preferences, and analyze how our service is used. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may be affected.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary. Account information is kept for up to 24 months after account closure. Usage and analytics data is retained for up to 24 months from collection.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us directly to make a request.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.
